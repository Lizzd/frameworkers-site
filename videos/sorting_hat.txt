The Crown of Ash

On the night the castle finally falls, the thousand-year-old Sorting Hat is lowered onto the last trembling first-year — but for the first time in a thousand years it refuses to name a house, sensing the darkness this child will one day become, and the old headmaster quietly lifts it away, leaving the child alone in the emptied hall: the very abandonment that will forge the monster. Stylized 3D cartoon animation (Pixar / DreamWorks look — clearly animated, non-photoreal), with English narration and English dialogue, English subtitles, and distant siege blasts, cracking stone, and foley.
