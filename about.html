Films born from
any input

FrameWorkers is an AI film director. It reads your intent, plans a custom production, and orchestrates a crew of specialist agents — each a craftsman for one stage of filmmaking — re-planning on the fly when a step falls short.

Different inputs call for different crews — a drama, an explainer, a news bulletin, a storybook, or a highlight cut from your own footage each get their own plan. No timelines, no editing — intent goes in, a finished film comes out.
